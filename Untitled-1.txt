PIRBView commands:

Book an advertisement on PIRBView scans:
/advert

Full security scan:
/s CA or /s NAME

Short info scan:
/i CA or /i NAME

Pull on-chain chart:
/c CA or /c NAME

Generate a EarlyBuyersMap:
/e CA or /e NAME (ERC20 & BEP20 only)

Generate a JeetMap:
/j CA or /j NAME (ERC20 & BEP20 only)

Generate a OverlapMap:
/o NAME1 NAME2 or /o CA1 CA2 (ERC20 only)

Generate a BubbleMap:
/b CA or /b NAME (ERC20 & BEP20 only)

Generate a WhaleMap:
/whale CA or /whale NAME (ERC20 & BEP20 only)

Generate a HoldersMap:
/hmap CA or /hmap NAME (ERC20 & BEP20 only)

Generate a AirdropMap:
/a CA or /a NAME (ERC20 & BEP20 only)

Check collected taxes:
/tax CA or /tax NAME (ERC20 & BEP20 only)

Team analysis:
/team CA or /team NAME (ERC20 only)

Top 15 holders analysis:
/h CA or /h NAME (ERC20 & BEP20 only)

Generate a wallet's Profit & Loss analysis:
/pnl WALLETADDRESS (ERC20 only)

On-chart Fibonacci levels & RSI generation:
/ta CA or /ta NAME

On-chart Moving Averages generation:
/ma CA or /ma NAME

On-chart Exponential Moving Averages generation:
/ema CA or /ema NAME

On-chart Bollinger Bands generation:
/bol CA or /bol NAME

On-chart Stochastik Movement Index generation:
/smi CA or /smi NAME

On-chart price prediction:
/predict CA or /predict NAME

See buy/sell trades on a token chart:
/tr CA/NAME WALLETADDRESS TIMEFRAME (ERC20 only)
Usage example: /tr PIRB WALLETADDRESS 4h

See the current ETH and gas prices:
/gas

See the Top 10 scanned tokens:
/top10

See the current fear and greed chart:
/fear

Generate a overall crypto heatmap:
/market
